Our Story

Sparked from a conversation with the Nobel Peace Laureate Mhd Yunus, we started with the hope of allowing anyone make a sustainable difference for communities in Asia. With as little as a $10 loan, your money can be given to as interest-free loans to help social enterprises, non-profits and impact organisations change lives.
